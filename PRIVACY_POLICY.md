**Privacy Policy for Game Score**

**Effective Date:** January 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Game Score ("we," "us," or "our") handles user information.

  * We do not use third-party SDKs for advertising, analytics, or any other tracking purposes.
  * Your data is not shared with any third parties.
2. Information From Google Play
  * When you download and use our app, Google provides us with limited, anonymous information such as your country, device model, operating system version, and screen resolution.
  * This information is used solely for ensuring the app functions correctly and is never linked to you personally or shared with others.
3. Data Security
  * Since we don't collect personal data, there is no data to secure, store, or lose.
4. Your Privacy
  * Your privacy is paramount. The app is designed to work offline and on-device, processing data only locally if needed for core features, with no data ever leaving your device.
5. Changes to This Policy
